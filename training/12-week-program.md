# 12-Week Training Program
**5-Day Push/Pull/Legs Split — Advanced Bro Split Variant**

---

## Program Philosophy

At 20 years of training, your primary gains come from **mechanical tension** (heavy compound work) and **metabolic stress** (volume, controlled rest). The split is designed so each muscle group is hit with high frequency:
- Chest: 2× per week (Monday + supplementary Thursday)
- Back: 2× per week (Tuesday + Friday)
- Legs: 1× per week dedicated (can add Saturday light work if needed)
- Shoulders: 2× per week (direct Thursday + indirect Monday)
- Arms: 2–3× per week via compound pressing/pulling

**Notation:** Sets × Rep Range | Rest Period

---

## PHASE 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

### Goal
Establish your working weights, lock in technique, build volume base. Do NOT ego-lift week 1. Use 70–80% of your true 1RM on compounds.

## PHASE 2 — Intensification (Weeks 5–8)

**Key changes from Phase 1:**
- Add 1 working set to all primary compound movements (bench, squat, deadlift, OHP, row)
- Increase load by 5–10% on exercises where you hit the top of the rep range consistently
- Reduce rest on isolation movements by 10–15 sec
- Cardio volume increases (see cardio protocol)

**Compound rep target shifts:**
- Main lifts (Set 1–2): 3–5 reps (heavier)
- Main lifts (Set 3–5): 6–8 reps (hypertrophy sets)

**Example — Day 1 Push A, Phase 2 Bench Press:**
- Set 1: 3 reps @ 90% effort
- Set 2: 3 reps @ 87% effort
- Sets 3–5: 6–8 reps @ 78–82% effort

All other exercises remain structurally the same — push the weights and squeeze the rest periods.

**New addition — Finisher superset (Days 1, 2, 4, 5):**
Add 2 rounds of a superset at the end of each session. Choose from:
- Push sessions: Cable lateral raise × 20 + push-up to failure
- Pull sessions: Face pull × 20 + band pull-apart × 20

---

## PHASE 3 — Peak Burn (Weeks 9–12)

**Key changes from Phase 2:**
- PR attempts on compound movements (Weeks 9 and 11)
- Drop sets introduced on 2 isolation exercises per session
- Rest-pause sets on machine exercises
- Cardio volume peaks (see cardio protocol)

**PR Week structure (Weeks 9 and 11 — apply to Bench, Squat, Deadlift, OHP):**
- Warm-up sets: 40%, 55%, 70%, 85% × 1
- Working set 1: 90–95% × 2–3 (near max)
- Working set 2: 80% × 6
- Working sets 3–4: 70% × 8–10

**Drop set protocol (choose 2 isolation exercises per session):**
- Complete your final set → immediately drop weight 25–30% → go to failure → drop again 25% → go to failure
- Example: Lateral raise 16 kg × 15 → drop to 10 kg × failure → drop to 6 kg × failure

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4) — Total ~35 min/week

| Day | Type | Duration | Parameters |

### HIIT Protocol (Treadmill)

**Phase 1–2 HIIT (20 min):**
- 3 min warm-up walk (5 km/h, flat)
- 8 × rounds: 30 sec sprint (10.5–12 km/h, flat) / 90 sec active recovery (5.5 km/h)
- 2 min cool-down walk
Total active intervals: 4 min | Total recovery: 12 min

**Phase 3 HIIT (20 min — intensified):**
- 2 min warm-up
- 10 × rounds: 30 sec sprint (12–13 km/h, incline 1.5%) / 60 sec walk (5 km/h)
- 2 min cool-down
Total active intervals: 5 min | Total recovery: 10 min

> **Critical rule:** If strength numbers drop more than 5% for 2+ consecutive sessions, cut one HIIT session immediately. Muscle retention is the priority.

## Progression Strategy

### The 2-for-2 Rule
If you complete the top of the rep range on your last set for 2 sessions in a row → add weight next session:
- Barbell compound (squat, deadlift, bench): +2.5 kg
- Dumbbell exercises: +2 kg (next available)
- Machine / cable: +2.5–5 kg depending on weight stack

### When You Stall
1. First, check sleep and nutrition — most stalls are recovery failures, not training failures
2. If calories have been at the deficit for 6+ weeks: implement a 2-week diet break at maintenance (TDEE) before returning to deficit
3. Rotate exercise variation (e.g., swap conventional deadlift for sumo, swap BB bench for DB bench for 4 weeks)
4. Deload — reduce volume by 40%, keep intensity the same

### Deload Week Protocol (Weeks 4, 8, 12)
- Perform same exercises
- Reduce all working sets from listed sets × reps to **2 sets × 8 reps** at ~60% of working weight
- No cardio beyond Saturday walk
- Sleep 8–9 hours — this is your growth window
