## 12-Week Phase Map

| Phase | Weeks | Deficit | Cardio Volume | Training Focus |
|-------|-------|---------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 — Foundation | 1–4 | −300 kcal | Low (35 min/wk) | Volume base, form refinement |
| 2 — Intensification | 5–8 | −400 kcal | Moderate (55 min/wk) | Heavier loads, added sets |
| 3 — Peak Burn | 9–12 | −500 kcal | High (80 min/wk) | PR attempts, maximum intensity |

Deload weeks: **Week 4, Week 8, Week 12** (reduce volume 40%, keep intensity)
